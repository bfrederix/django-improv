Admin Quick Tutorial
  • Create cast Players that will be featured during votes in your show.
    Create a Player
  • Create Suggestion Pools that give the audience the ability to enter suggestions once a Show is created.
    Create a Suggestion Pool
  • Create Vote Types that determine how votes will appear during your Show.
    Create a Vote Type
  • Once you have created a Vote Type, you can now create a Show that packages your Players and Vote Types and allows your audience to interact during your live show.
    Create a Show
* Note: Players or Suggestion Pools are not required, but you must have either Players or a Suggestion Pool to create a Show.
Admin Show Tutorial
  • Once your Show is created, open the "Show Display" page on your projector/screen for your audience to see and interact with.
  • To start live Show votes, open the "Show Controller" page separately from your "Show Display" page and click any of the "Start Vote" buttons.
* Note: The "Show Lock" exists to ensure audience members are redirected to the "Live Voting Page". Anytime a vote is started, the "Show Lock" is automatically turned on and any link on your Channel will take your users to the "Live Voting Page". If you need them to enter more Suggestions at any time, make sure you set the show lock to "Unlocked".
