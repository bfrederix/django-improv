Features


Free

  • Create Show Channels
  • Audience Suggestions can be entered and upvoted before the show
  • Live Voting for suggestions by audience members during the show
  • Create cast Players with uploaded photos
  • Create Suggestion Pools for audience members to submit their suggestions to (Character Titles, Locations, Objects, etc.)
  • Create Voting Types that allow audience members to vote for players or suggestions during the show
  • Show Recaps are available for viewing suggestions or cast players that were voted for in each show

Premium

  • Facebook or Google log in for audience members
  • User E-mails are collected and available for export when audience members login with their social media accounts and can be used for promoting / marketing
  • Leaderboards track logged in audience members that receive votes for their suggestions
  • User Medals are awarded to audience members that meet specific goals in the show
  • User History pages track the suggestion history and achievements of logged in audience members

About


Dumpedit is a free to use improv voting app. It's designed to allow audiences to be more engaged in your improv shows.

Whether you're doing a long-form or short-form show, Dumpedit gets your audience involved and participating in the action!
